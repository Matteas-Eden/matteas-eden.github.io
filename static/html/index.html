Bakugan, brawl!

This is Bakugan Rerolled, a free fangame designed to simulate and replicate the battle system featured in Bakugan Battle Brawlers. The goal of this game is to be able to replicate (almost) every battle that happened in the show, across all 52 episodes of the original run. There is also the ability to play through an original story involving all of the main cast.
